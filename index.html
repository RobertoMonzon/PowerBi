Interactive 3D Dental Dashboard

I have developed an interactive 3D dental dashboard that provides a comprehensive view of various dental metrics and statistics. This tool allows users to explore individual teeth in detail and access data on the number of patients treated for specific dental issues related to each tooth. Additionally, the dashboard includes:

  • A feature to track how many patients each of my dentists has attended.
  • A line chart to calculate monthly revenue.
  • A line chart to monitor the number of patients per month.
  • A pie chart to display the gender distribution of patients.
  • Four summary cards with key information.

These features make the dashboard a valuable resource for dental professionals to analyze and track patient care and business performance efficiently.

Butcher Shop Performance Dashboard

I have developed a unique dashboard in Power BI that simulates the layout of a butcher shop's chalkboard. This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of key metrics and performance indicators for your butcher shop operations.

  • Cow-shaped Graph: Interactive graph allowing users to select different cuts of meat, providing detailed information such as sales volume, pricing, and other relevant data for each cut.
  • Sales Table: A table displaying the quantity of sales, income, expenses, and profit, providing a clear breakdown of financial performance.
  • Mexico Map: An interactive map of Mexico highlighting regions with the highest concentration of customers.
  • Bar Chart: A bar chart showcasing the performance of your six branches.
  • Performance Message: A dynamic message that changes based on the overall performance of your butcher shop.
  • Top Selling Cut Card: A card displaying the best-selling cut of meat.

Company Health and Safety Dashboard

I have developed an interactive dashboard for a company that provides a comprehensive view of various health and safety metrics. This tool includes navigational buttons with icons representing different parts of the body, allowing users to explore clinical results for each specific area. The dashboard offers the following features:

  • A feature to track how many patients each of our healthcare providers has attended.
  • A line chart to calculate monthly revenue.
  • A line chart to monitor the number of patients per month.
  • A pie chart to display the gender distribution of patients.
  • Four summary cards with key information.
  • A comparison tool for analyzing periodic exam results within the company.
